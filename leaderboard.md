# Leaderboard Policy

No public leaderboard is active for v0.2.

Candidate-label results may be reported as diagnostics only. A credible
leaderboard requires:

- adjudicated human gold labels;
- a private or delayed holdout for scored submissions;
- immutable dataset versioning;
- detector submission metadata;
- per-category metrics with confidence intervals;
- contamination and retuning disclosures.
